Oack is an uptime and performance monitoring platform with deep TCP-level
telemetry, multi-channel alerting, and AI-assisted troubleshooting via MCP.
Run checkers on your own infrastructure, get notified in seconds, and
diagnose issues all the way down to the network layer.
