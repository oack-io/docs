---

Get your first monitor running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [app.oack.io](https://app.oack.io/signup) and create your first team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a monitor">
    Enter the URL you want to watch, pick an HTTP method, set the check interval, and choose a checker region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure alerts">
    Create alert channels (Slack, Discord, Telegram, PagerDuty, Email, or Webhook) and link them to your monitor. You'll be notified within seconds when something goes wrong.
  </Step>
</Steps>
