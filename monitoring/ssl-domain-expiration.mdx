---
title: "SSL & Domain Expiration"
description: "Automatic SSL certificate and domain registration expiration monitoring."
---

Oack automatically monitors SSL certificate and domain registration expiration for every active monitor. A daily sweep checks:

- **SSL certificates** — TLS handshake reads the leaf certificate's expiration date.
- **Domain registration** — RDAP protocol (the ICANN standard replacement for WHOIS) checks registration expiry.

Notifications are sent through your linked alert channels at configurable thresholds (default: 30, 14, 7, and 1 days before expiration). Duplicate alerts are suppressed until the next threshold.

<Note>Available on Pro and Business plans.</Note>
