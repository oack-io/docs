---
title: "Probe Aggregation"
description: "Automatic time-bucket aggregation for long time ranges with statistical functions."
---

For time ranges longer than 12 hours, probes are automatically aggregated into time buckets using SQL-level statistical functions.

## Aggregation functions

Available functions: `avg`, `median`, `min`, `max`, `p75`, `p90`, `p95`, `p99`.

## Bucket sizing

Bucket size scales with range:

| Time Range | Bucket Size |
|-----------|-------------|
| 12–24 hours | 5 minutes |
| 24h – 7 days | 30 minutes |
| 7 – 30 days | 2 hours |
| 30 – 90 days | 6 hours |
| 90+ days | 12 hours |

Each bucket includes all six timing phases, probe count, and error count. Maximum 1,000 buckets per query.
